About Us

Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal (JSPM) was established in 1998 with the objective of creating centers of excellence for education in the field of Engineering,
Medical, Pharmacy, Management and Computers. The management envisions that a social transformation can be brought about through the medium of dynamic
education. The students are infused with an attitude of leading and winning without compromising on the moral and ethical values, nurturing them into holistically
developed individuals.

Today JSPM is an agglomerate of more than 78 institutes spread across six campuses providing quality ranging from "Pre-school to Doctoral Research" for over 60000+
students in the metropolitan region of Pune.


The Journey of JSPM


1998 JSPM Started     -  The Formation of Public Charitable Trust,"JSPM" with the vision to establlish quality educational system
2001 Tathawade campus     - The first unit of jspm started in the year 2001 under the Rajashree Shahu College of Engineering at the Tathawade
2013 Award Receives     - Receive the award from Pune university at best professional college also NBA accreditated
2014 Barshi campus     - All the campuses are having quality Educational Institutes schools and colleges with excellent infrastructure
2018 JSPM campuses     - 7 campuses, 78 institutes, 500 buses, 60000+ students
2023 Wagholi campus     - JSPM University Wagholi Pune
