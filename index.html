Leadership

In 1998, Prof. (Dr.) T. J. Sawant embarked on an ambitious journey by founding the "Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal" (JSPM),
aiming to establish high-quality educational institutions in Pune. His intellectual acumen shines through successful acquisitions of
TSSM and SBERCT Group of Institutes. Also, as Chairman of Bhairavnath Sugar Works, he demonstrates commitment to diverse
sectors. Since 1998, under Prof. Sawant's exceptional leadership, JSPM has thrived, making significant contributions to Pune's
educational landscape and fostering social and economic development. His energy and vision continue to shape the region's future,
earning him a well-deserved reputation as a dynamic leader. JSPM's expansion across Pune's suburbs, the establishment of private
university illustrate Prof. (Dr.) T. J. Sawant's unwavering commitment to multifaceted development. Despite its relatively brief
existence, JSPM boasts a remarkable legacy of achievements and success stories.
