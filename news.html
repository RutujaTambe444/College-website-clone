News and Events


JSPM University inks MoU with ARAI

JSPM University and the Automotive Research Association of India (ARAI) have recently formalized an
ambitious collaboration by signing five Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs). This significant partnership
is designed to introduce a range of undergraduate and post-graduate programs within the dynamic and ever
-evolving technology domain.

One of the driving forces behind this collaboration is the pressing need to meet the escalating demand for a
highly skilled workforce in the technology sector. As industries continue to advance at a rapid pace, there is a
growing requirement for individuals who possess cutting-edge knowledge and expertise in various
technological fields.

In response to this demand, JSPM University and ARAI have joined forces to forge a mutually beneficial
relationship. This partnership extends beyond traditional educational boundaries, focusing on the dual
pillars of joint research and academic collaborations. By combining their resources, expertise, and
infrastructure, these institutions aim to not only provide top-tier education but also contribute significantly
to technological advancements through research initiatives.

In essence, this collaboration serves as a beacon of innovation and progress in the technology domain,
ensuring that future generations of professionals are equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to
thrive in an ever-changing technological landscape.


Visit of Amirkhan Alim Modi, Principal Engineer, Whirlpool of India Ltd.

Amirkhan Alim Modi, Principal Engineer, Whirlpool of India Ltd. During the visit of Amirkhan Alim Modi,
Principal Engineer from Whirlpool of India Ltd., several key discussions took place regarding collaboration
opportunities. One notable revelation was the existence of a Design Unit in Kharadi, housing an impressive
team of 1000 B.E./B.Tech engineers. This unit is responsible for designing a remarkable 2.5 to 3
million designs annually, making it the largest design center for Whirlpool worldwide. Additionally, Whirlpool
operates a manufacturing plant in Ranjangaon, employing over 500 Diploma holders. As a result of the fruitful
meeting, We requested Mr. Modi to arrange a visit to the Kharadi Office, aiming to further explore potential collaborations.


International Yoga Day Celebration at JSPM University, Pune

The celebration of International Yoga Day at JSPM University, Pune, was a resounding success, bringing
together students, staff, and esteemed dignitaries. Dr. R. S. Deshpande and Mr. Vishal Chaudhary, along with
teachers from the Art of Living, enlightened the audience about the benefits of yoga and meditation. The
demonstration of various yoga exercises by Priti Survase and Priyanka Mehara encouraged active
participation. Dattatraya Ozarkar emphasized the importance of yoga for students, promoting concentration
and overall well-being. Under the guidance of Dr. B. B. Ahuja, the Vice-Chancellor, the event showcased unity
and a commitment to personal growth. The celebration underscored the significance of yoga in fostering a
healthy and positive environment within the university community.


Project Exhibition and Competition at JSPM University

The Project Exhibition and Competition organized by JSPM University's School of Science and Technology,
Pune, showcased the exceptional talents of final year students. Under the guidance of Dr. B. B. Ahuja, Vice
Chancellor, and Dr. R. S. Deshpande, the event brought unity and collaboration among all schools. Industry
experts evaluated the groundbreaking projects, providing valuable feedback and opportunities for future
enhancement. This event served as a catalyst for innovation, skill development, and industry connections,
empowering students and shaping their promising careers. It was a celebration of outstanding
achievements and a testament to the university's commitment to nurturing talent and fostering excellence.


MoU with Indovance Ltd.

The signing of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between JSPM University and Indovance Ltd.
marks a significant collaboration for student exchange and collaborative activities. The MoU envisions a
range of initiatives, including student exchange programs, expert talks, and internships, providing students
with practical exposure and industry insights. Indovance Ltd.'s expertise in design consultancy for civil and
mechanical engineering adds immense value to the partnership. This collaboration exemplifies a
commitment to innovation, skill development, and industry readiness, nurturing the growth of future
engineers. The MoU sets the stage for transformative experiences, empowering JSPM University's
engineering students and positioning them as future industry leaders.
